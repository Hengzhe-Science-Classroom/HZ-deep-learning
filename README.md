## What You'll Learn

- Feedforward networks and backpropagation
- Regularization and optimization
- Convolutional neural networks
- Recurrent networks and attention
- Generative models: VAEs, GANs, diffusion
- Transformers and large language models

- Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio & Aaron Courville, *Deep Learning* (MIT Press, free online)
- Simon J.D. Prince, *Understanding Deep Learning* (MIT Press)
- Dive into Deep Learning (d2l.ai, interactive textbook)

- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
